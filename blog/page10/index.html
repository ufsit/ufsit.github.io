Meeting - November 13, 2013 | Reverse Engineering - Wireshark

  • meeting announcement

Hey SITers!

This Wednesday we will apply our knowledge of Wireshark to reverse engineer network protocols and write scripts to exploit the protocol. We’ll also be talking about the real world applications of being able to write these exploits.

[Time and Location]

Wednesday 7:00pm-9:00pm

1151 McCarty Hall A

[Stuff to Bring]

  • Laptop with Kali Linux (bare metal or VM) and an ethernet port

[Ethics Agreement]

If you haven’t yet signed an the ethics agreement posted at http://ufhack.org/ethics/, please bring a copy of the agreement, printed out and signed, to Wednesday’s meeting.

Meeting - October 23, 2013 | SwampSec

  • meeting announcement

This week, SIT is taking a trip to SwampSec <http://swampsec.org/>! If you don’t know what SwampSec is, it’s a gathering of infosec professionals that takes place on the last Wednesday (7-9pm) of the month downtown at Stubbies & Steins <http://stubbiesandsteins.com/>

We’ll be carpooling so if you would like to carpool there, show up at McCarty at 6:45 and we’ll all go over together.

Please note, this meeting is going to take place at Stubbies. We are meeting up at McCarty to travel there, but the meeting will be at Stubbies.

If you have any questions, let us know!
